Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int \frac{x}{x^2+2} dx }$
Solution
Make the following substitution:
$\eqalign{ u &= x^2 + 2 \\ du &= 2x dx \rightarrow \frac{du}{2} = x dx }$
to simplify the integral:
$\eqalign{ \int \frac{x}{x^2+2} dx &= \int \frac{du}{2u} \\ &= \frac{1}{2} \int u^{-1} du \\ &= \frac{1}{2} \ln|u| + C \\ &= \frac{1}{2} \ln|x^2 + 2| + C }$
