Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int_e^3 \frac{\ln(x)}{x} dx }$
Solution
Make the following substitution:
$\eqalign{ u &= \ln(x) \\ du &= \frac{dx}{x} }$
to simplify the integral:
$\eqalign{ \int_e^3 \frac{\ln(x)}{x} dx &= \int_{\ln(e)}^{\ln(3)} u du \\ &= \frac{u^2}{2} \Big|_1^{\ln(3)} \\ &= \frac{\ln^2(3) - 1}{2} }$
