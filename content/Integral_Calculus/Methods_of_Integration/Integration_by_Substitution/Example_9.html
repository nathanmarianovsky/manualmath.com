Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} dx }$
Solution
Make the following substitution:
$\eqalign{ u &= e^x + e^{-x} \\ du &= \Big( e^x - e^{-x} \Big) dx }$
to simplify the integral:
$\eqalign{ \int \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} dx &= \int \frac{du}{u} \\ &= \ln|u| + C \\ &= \ln\Big| e^x + e^{-x} \Big| + C }$
