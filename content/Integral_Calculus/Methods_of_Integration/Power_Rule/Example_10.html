Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int_0^2 \Big( (\pi x)^2 - e^{10} \Big) dx }$
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \int_a^b x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \Big|_a^b }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \int_0^2 \Big( (\pi x)^2 - e^{10} \Big) dx &= \pi^2 \int_0^2 x^2 dx - e^{10} \int_0^2 dx \\ &= \Big( \frac{\pi^2}{3}x^3 - e^{10}x \Big) \Big|_0^2 \\ &= \frac{8\pi^2}{3} - 2e^{10} }$
