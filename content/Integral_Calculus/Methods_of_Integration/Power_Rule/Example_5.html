Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int_3^1 (x+2)(x-2) dx }$
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \int_a^b x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \Big|_a^b }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \int_3^1 (x+2)(x-2) dx &= -\int_1^3 \Big( x^2 - 4 \Big) dx \\ &= - \int_1^3 x^2 dx + 4 \int_1^3 dx \\ &= \Big[ -\frac{x^3}{3} + 4x \Big] \Big|_1^3 \\ &= \Big( -9 + 12 \Big) - \Big( -\frac{1}{3} + 4 \Big) \\ &= -\frac{2}{3} }$
