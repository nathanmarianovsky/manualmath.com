Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int_1^e \frac{3+x}{x} dx }$
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \int_a^b x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \Big|_a^b }$
and:
$\eqalign{ \int_a^b x^{-1} dx = \ln|x| \Big|_a^b }$
when $n = -1$. So:
$\eqalign{ \int_1^e \frac{3+x}{x} dx &= 3 \int_1^e \frac{dx}{x} + \int_1^e dx \\ &= 3 \cdot \Big( \ln|x| \Big|_1^e \Big) + \Big( x \Big|_1^e \Big) \\ &= 3 \Big( \ln(e) - \ln(1) \Big) + \Big( e - 1 \Big) \\ &= 2 + e }$
