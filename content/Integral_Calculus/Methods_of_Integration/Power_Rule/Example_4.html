Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int_1^2 (x+3)^2 dx }$
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \int_a^b x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \Big|_a^b }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \int_1^2 (x+3)^2 dx &= \int_1^2 \Big( x^2 + 6x + 9 \Big) dx \\ &= \int_1^2 x^2 dx + 6 \int_1^2 x dx + 9 \int_1^2 dx \\ &= \Big[ \frac{x^3}{3} + 3x^2 + 9x \Big] \Big|_1^2 \\ &= \Big( \frac{8}{3} + 12 + 18 \Big) - \Big( \frac{1}{3} + 3 + 9 \Big) \\ &= \frac{61}{3} }$
