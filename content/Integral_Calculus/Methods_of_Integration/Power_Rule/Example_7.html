Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int \Big( t\sqrt{t} + \frac{1}{t\sqrt{t}} \Big) dt }$
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \int t^n dt = \frac{t^{n+1}}{n+1} + C }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \int \Big( t\sqrt{t} + \frac{1}{t\sqrt{t}} \Big) dt &= \int t^\frac{3}{2} dt + \int t^{-\frac{3}{2}} dt \\ &= \frac{2}{5}t^\frac{5}{2} - 2t^{-\frac{1}{2}} + C }$
