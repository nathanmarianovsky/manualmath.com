Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int \Big( \frac{y^2 - 1}{y} \Big)^2 dy }$
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \int y^n dt = \frac{y^{n+1}}{n+1} + C }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \int \Big( \frac{y^2 - 1}{y} \Big)^2 dy &= \int \frac{(y^2 - 1)^2}{y^2} dy \\ &= \int \frac{y^4 - 2y^2 + 1}{y^2} dy \\ &= \int y^2 dy - 2 \int dy + \int y^{-2} dy \\ &= \frac{1}{3}y^3 - 2y - y^{-1} + C }$
