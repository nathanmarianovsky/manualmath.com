Problem
Evaluate the following:
$\eqalign{ \int \Big( \frac{3}{x^2} + x^7 \Big) dx }$
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \int \Big( \frac{3}{x^2} + x^7 \Big) dx &= 3 \int x^{-2} dx + \int x^7 dx \\ &= 3 \cdot \frac{x^{-1}}{-1} + \frac{x^8}{8} + C \\ &= -\frac{3}{x} + \frac{x^8}{8} + C \\ }$
