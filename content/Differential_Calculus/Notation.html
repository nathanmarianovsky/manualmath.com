Notation

Here you will find all the notation used throughout the study of differential calculus and its corresponding meaning:
$y'$ or $y'(x)$ Lagrange's Notation for the First Derivative
$y''$ or $y''(x)$ Lagrange's Notation for the Second Derivative
$y'''$ or $y'''(x)$ Lagrange's Notation for the Third Derivative
$y^{(n)}$ or $y^{(n)}(x)$ The $n$th Derivative
$\frac{d^ny}{dx^n}$ Leibniz's Notation for the $n$th Derivative
$\frac{d^ny}{dx^n} \Big|_{x = x_0}$ Leibniz's Notation for the $n$th Derivative Evaluted at $x = x_0$
