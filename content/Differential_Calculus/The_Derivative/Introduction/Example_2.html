Problem
Given the function:
$\eqalign{ f(x) = \frac{1}{x} }$
Determine $f'(7)$.
Solution
$\eqalign{ f'(7) &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{f(7 + h) - f(7)}{h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\frac{1}{7 + h} - \frac{1}{7}}{h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\frac{7 - (7 + h)}{7(7 + h)}}{h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} -\frac{1}{7(7 + h)} \\ &= -\frac{1}{49} }$
