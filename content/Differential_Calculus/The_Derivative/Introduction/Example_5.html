Problem
Determine:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dt} \frac{t}{t + 1} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dt} \frac{t}{t + 1} &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\frac{t + h}{t + h + 1} - \frac{t}{t + 1}}{h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\frac{(t + h)(t + 1) - t(t + h + 1)}{(t + h + 1)(t + 1)}}{h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{(t^2 + t + ht + h) - (t^2 + th + t)}{h(t + h + 1)(t + 1)} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{1}{(t + h + 1)(t + 1)} \\ &= \frac{1}{(t + 1)^2} }$
