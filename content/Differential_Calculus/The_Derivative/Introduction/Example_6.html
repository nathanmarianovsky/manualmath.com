Problem
Determine the derivative of:
$\eqalign{ f(x) = \sqrt{x + 1} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \frac{df}{dx} &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\sqrt{x + h + 1} - \sqrt{x + 1}}{h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{x + h + 1 - (x + 1)}{h(\sqrt{x + h + 1} + \sqrt{x + 1})} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x + h + 1} + \sqrt{x + 1}} \\ &= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{x + 1}} }$
