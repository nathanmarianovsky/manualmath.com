Problem
Find the derivative of:
$\eqalign{ h(x) = \sin(x)\tan(x) }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ &\frac{d}{dx} h(x) \\ &= \frac{d}{dx} \Big( \sin(x)\tan(x) \Big) \\ &= \frac{d}{dx} \Big( \sin(x) \Big) \cdot \tan(x) \\ & \hspace{.4cm} + \sin(x) \cdot \frac{d}{dx} \Big( \tan(x) \Big) \\ &= \cos(x)\tan(x) + \sin(x)\sec^2(x) }$
