Problem
Find the derivative of:
$\eqalign{ h(x) = (5 + x^2)(7 + x + x^5) }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ &\frac{d}{dx} h(x) \\ &= \frac{d}{dx} \Big( (5 + x^2)(7 + x + x^5) \Big) \\ &= \frac{d}{dx} \Big( 5 + x^2 \Big) \cdot (7 + x + x^5) \\ & \hspace{.4cm} + (5 + x^2) \cdot \frac{d}{dx} \Big( 7 + x + x^5 \Big) \\ &= 2x(7 + x + x^5) + (5 + x^2)(1 + 5x^4) }$
