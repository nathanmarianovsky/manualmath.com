Problem
Find the derivative of $h(x) = 3x^2\sin(x)$.
Solution
According to the product rule:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} f(x)g(x) = \frac{d}{dx} \Big( f(x) \Big) \cdot g(x) + f(x) \cdot \frac{d}{dx} \Big( g(x) \Big) }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} h(x) &= \frac{d}{dx} \Big( 3x^2\sin(x) \Big) \\ &= \frac{d}{dx} \Big( 3x^2 \Big) \cdot \sin(x) + 3x^2 \cdot \frac{d}{dx} \Big( \sin(x) \Big) \\ &= 6x + \cos(x) }$
