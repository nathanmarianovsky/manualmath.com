Problem
Given:
$\eqalign{ e^{xy} = e^{4x} - e^{5y} }$
Determine $y'(x)$.
Solution
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} e^{xy} &= \frac{d}{dx} \Big[ e^{4x} - e^{5y} \Big] \\ (y + xy')e^{xy} &= 4e^{4x} - 5y'e^{5y} \\ y'(xe^{xy} + 5e^{5y}) &= 4e^{4x} - ye^{xy} \\ y' &= \frac{4e^{4x} - ye^{xy}}{xe^{xy} + 5e^{5y}} }$
