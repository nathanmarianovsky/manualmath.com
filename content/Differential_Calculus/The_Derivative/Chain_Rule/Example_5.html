Problem
Prove the following identity:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{df_{n + 1}} f_1(f_2(f_3(\dots f_{n - 1}(f_n(f_{n + 1})))) = \prod_{i = 1}^n \frac{df_i}{df_{i + 1}} }$
assuming that for each value of $i$, $f_i(x)$ is differentiable at $f_{i + 1}(x)$.
Solution
This is nothing more than an extension to the chain rule, one that handles the composition of not just two functions, but rather $n + 1$ of them. For a formal proof, consider using induction. For the base case let $n = 2$:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{df_3} f_1(f_2(f_3)) &= \prod_{i = 1}^2 \frac{df_i}{df_{i + 1}} \\ \frac{df_1}{df_2} \cdot \frac{df_2}{df_3} &= \frac{df_1}{df_2} \cdot \frac{df_2}{df_3} }$
which holds true since we accept the standard chain rule. Now we assume that given statement and try to show that it holds true for $n + 1$:
$\eqalign{ &\frac{d}{df_{n + 2}} f_1(f_2(f_3(\dots f_{n + 1}(f_{n + 2})))) \\ &= \prod_{i = 1}^{n + 1} \frac{df_i}{df_{i + 1}} \\ &= \prod_{i = 1}^n \frac{df_i}{df_{i + 1}} \cdot \frac{df_{n + 1}}{df_{n + 2}} \\ &= \frac{d}{df_{n + 1}} f_1(\dots f_{n - 1}(f_n(f_{n + 1}))) \cdot \frac{df_{n + 1}}{df_{n + 2}} \\ &= \frac{d}{df_{n + 2}} f_1(f_2(f_3(\dots f_{n + 1}(f_{n + 2})))) }$
where the last step involves reversing the standard chain rule. While a formal proof is always important, it just as important to understand how the statement is derived. In this scenario we can derive the derivative of a composition of $n + 1$ functions by recursively using the standard chain rule:
$\eqalign{ &\frac{d}{df_{n + 1}} f_1(f_2(f_3(\dots f_{n - 1}(f_n(f_{n + 1})))) \\ &= \frac{df_1}{df_2} \cdot \frac{d}{df_{n + 1}} f_2(f_3(\dots f_n(f_{n + 1}))) \\ &= \frac{df_1}{df_2} \cdot \frac{df_2}{df_3} \cdot \frac{d}{df_{n + 1}} f_3(\dots f_n(f_{n + 1})) \\ &= \frac{df_1}{df_2} \cdot \frac{df_2}{df_3} \cdot \frac{df_3}{df_4} \cdot \frac{d}{df_{n + 1}} f_4(\dots f_n(f_{n + 1})) \\ &\vdots \\ &= \prod_{i = 1}^{n - 1} \frac{df_i}{df_{i + 1}} \cdot \frac{d}{df_{n + 1}} f_n(f_{n + 1}) \\ &= \prod_{i = 1}^n \frac{df_i}{df_{i + 1}} \cdot \frac{d}{df_{n + 1}} f_{n + 1} \\ &= \prod_{i = 1}^n \frac{df_i}{df_{i + 1}} }$
