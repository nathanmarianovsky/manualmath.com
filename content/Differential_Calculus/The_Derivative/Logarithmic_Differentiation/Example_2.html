Problem
Find the derivative of:
$\eqalign{ y(x) = x^{e^x} }$
Solution
Take the logarithm of both sides and differentiate:
$\eqalign{ y(x) &= x^{e^x} \\ \ln(y(x)) &= \ln\Big( x^{e^x} \Big) \\ \ln(y(x)) &= e^x\ln(x) \\ \frac{d}{dx} \ln(y(x)) &= \frac{d}{dx} e^x\ln(x) \\ \frac{y'(x)}{y(x)} &= e^x\ln(x) + \frac{e^x}{x} \\ y'(x) &= y(x)\Big( e^x\ln(x) + \frac{e^x}{x} \Big) \\ &= x^{e^x} \Big( e^x\ln(x) + \frac{e^x}{x} \Big) }$
