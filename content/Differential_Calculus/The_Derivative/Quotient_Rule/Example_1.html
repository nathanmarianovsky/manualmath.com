Problem
Find the derivative of:
$\eqalign{ h(x) = \frac{2x}{\sin(x)} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ &\frac{d}{dx} h(x) \\ &= \frac{d}{dx} \Bigg( \frac{2x}{\sin(x)} \Bigg) \\ &= \frac{\frac{d}{dx} \Big( 2x \Big) \cdot \sin(x) - 2x \cdot \frac{d}{dx} \Big( \sin(x) \Big)}{\sin^2(x)} \\ &= \frac{2\sin(x) - 2x\cos(x)}{\sin^2(x)} }$
