Problem
Find the derivative of $f(x) = \frac{1}{\pi x}$.
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} x^n = nx^{n-1} \hspace{.3cm} \text{where} \hspace{.3cm} n \in \mathbb{R} }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} f(x) &= \frac{d}{dx} \frac{1}{\pi x} \\ &= \frac{1}{\pi} \frac{d}{dx} x^{-1} \\ &= -\frac{x^{-2}}{\pi} \\ &= -\frac{1}{\pi x^2} }$
