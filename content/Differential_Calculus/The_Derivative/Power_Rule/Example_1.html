Problem
Find the derivative of:
$\eqalign{ f(x) = 3x^2 }$
Solution
According to the power rule:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} x^n = nx^{n-1} \hspace{.3cm} \text{where} \hspace{.3cm} n \in \mathbb{R} }$
so:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} f(x) &= \frac{d}{dx} \Big( 3x^2 \Big) \\ &= 3 \frac{d}{dx} \Big( x^2 \Big) \\ &= 3 \cdot 2x \\ &= 6x }$
