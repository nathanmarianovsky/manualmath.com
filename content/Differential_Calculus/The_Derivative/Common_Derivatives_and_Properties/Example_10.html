Problem
Prove:
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} \ln\Big( \prod_{i = 1}^n (f_i(x))^{\alpha_i} \Big) = \sum_{i = 1}^n \alpha_i \frac{d}{dx} \ln(f_i(x)) }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \frac{d}{dx} \ln\Big( \prod_{i = 1}^n (f_i(x))^{\alpha_i} \Big) &= \frac{d}{dx} \sum_{i = 1}^n \ln((f_i(x))^{\alpha_i}) \\ &= \frac{d}{dx} \sum_{i = 1}^n \alpha_i \ln(f_i(x)) \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n \frac{d}{dx} \alpha_i \ln(f_i(x)) \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n \alpha_i \frac{d}{dx} \ln(f_i(x)) }$
