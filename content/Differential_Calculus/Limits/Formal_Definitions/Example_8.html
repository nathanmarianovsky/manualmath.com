Problem
Prove the $\textit{Triangle Inequality}$:
$\eqalign{ |x + y| \leq |x| + |y| \hspace{.3cm} \text{where} \hspace{.3cm} x,y \in \mathbb{R} }$
Solution
Begin by noting that:
$\eqalign{ |x + y|^2 = (x + y)^2 }$
Now by expanding the right side and using properties of the absolute value:
$\eqalign{ |x + y|^2 &= (x + y)^2 \\ &= x^2 + 2xy + y^2 \\ &= |x|^2 + 2xy + |y|^2 \\ &\leq |x|^2 + 2|xy| + |y|^2 \\ &= |x|^2 + 2|x||y| + |y|^2 \\ &= (|x| + |y|)^2 }$
We finally arrive at:
$\eqalign{ |x + y|^2 &\leq (|x| + |y|)^2 \\ |x + y| &\leq |x| + |y| }$
