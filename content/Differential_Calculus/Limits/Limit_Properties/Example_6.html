Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 5} \frac{x^2 - 25}{x^2 + x - 30} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 5} \frac{x^2 - 25}{x^2 + x - 30} &= \lim_{x \rightarrow 5} \frac{(x - 5)(x + 5)}{(x + 6)(x - 5)} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow 5} \frac{x + 5}{x + 6} \\ &= \Big[ \lim_{x \rightarrow 5} (x + 5) \Big] \Big[ \lim_{x \rightarrow 5} (x + 6) \Big]^{-1} \\ &= \frac{10}{11} }$
