Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{h \rightarrow 4} \frac{h - \sqrt{3h + 4}}{4 - h} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ &\lim_{h \rightarrow 4} \frac{h - \sqrt{3h + 4}}{4 - h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 4} \frac{h - \sqrt{3h + 4}}{4 - h} \cdot \frac{h + \sqrt{3h + 4}}{h + \sqrt{3h + 4}} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 4} \frac{h^2 - 3h - 4}{(4 - h)(h + \sqrt{3h + 4})} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 4} \frac{(h - 4)(h + 1)}{-(h - 4)(h + \sqrt{3h + 4})} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 4} -\frac{h + 1}{h + \sqrt{3h + 4}} \\ &= -\Big[ \lim_{h \rightarrow 4} (h + 1) \Big] \Big[ \lim_{h \rightarrow 4} (h + \sqrt{3h + 4}) \Big]^{-1} \\ &= -\frac{5}{8} }$
