Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{2(-3 + h)^2 - 18}{h} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{2(-3 + h)^2 - 18}{h} &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{2(9 - 6h + h^2) - 18}{h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{h^2 - 12h}{h} \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} (h - 12) \\ &= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} h - \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} 12 \\ &= -12 }$
