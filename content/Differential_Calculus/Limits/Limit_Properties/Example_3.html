Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} (x^3 + 3)\csc\Big( x - \frac{\pi}{2} \Big) }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} (x^3 + 3)\csc\Big( x - \frac{\pi}{2} \Big) &= \Big[ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} x^3 + \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} 3 \Big] \cdot \Big[ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} \csc\Big( x - \frac{\pi}{2} \Big) \Big] \\ &= (3)(-1) \\ &= -3 }$
