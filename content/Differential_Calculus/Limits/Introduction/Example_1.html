Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 2} f(x) }$
where you are given the graph of the function:
Solution
Observing the graph shows that from the left side the function approaches 1, but from the right side approaches -6. Since these two are not equal:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 2} f(x) = \text{DNE} }$
