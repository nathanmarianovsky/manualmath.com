Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} f(x) }$
where you are given the graph of the function:
Solution
Observing the graph shows that from the left side the function approaches 4, but from the right side approaches 9. Since these two are not equal:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} f(x) = \text{DNE} }$
