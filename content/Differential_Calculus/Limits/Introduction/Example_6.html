Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} f(x) \hspace{.3cm} \text{where} \hspace{.3cm} f(x) = \begin{cases} -x + 1 & x < 0 \\ 10 & x = 0 \\ x + 1 & x > 0 \end{cases} }$
using a numerical approach.
Solution
First the table of values:
$\textbf{x}$ $\textbf{f(x)}$
$-0.100$ $1.100$
$-0.010$ $1.010$
$-0.001$ $1.001$
$0.001$ $1.001$
$0.010$ $1.010$
$0.100$ $1.100$
which provides us with enough evidence to state that:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 0} f(x) = 1 }$
