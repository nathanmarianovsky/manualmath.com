Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 2} \frac{x^2 + 4x - 12}{x^2 - 2x} }$
using an analytical approach.
Solution
By simplifying:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 2} \frac{x^2 + 4x - 12}{x^2 - 2x} &= \lim_{x \rightarrow 2} \frac{(x - 2)(x + 6)}{x(x - 2)} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow 2} \frac{x + 6}{x} \\ &= 4 }$
