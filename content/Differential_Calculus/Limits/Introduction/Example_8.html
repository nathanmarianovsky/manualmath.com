Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow 4} \frac{x - \sqrt{3x + 4}}{4 - x} }$
using an analytical approach.
Solution
By simplifying:
$\eqalign{ &\lim_{x \rightarrow 4} \frac{x - \sqrt{3x + 4}}{4 - x} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow 4} \frac{x - \sqrt{3x + 4}}{4 - x} \cdot \frac{x + \sqrt{3x + 4}}{t + \sqrt{3x + 4}} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow 4} \frac{x^2 - 3x - 4}{(4 - x)(x + \sqrt{3x + 4})} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow 4} \frac{(x - 4)(x + 1)}{-(x - 4)(x + \sqrt{3x + 4})} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow 4} -\frac{x + 1}{x + \sqrt{3x + 4}} \\ &= -\frac{5}{8} }$
