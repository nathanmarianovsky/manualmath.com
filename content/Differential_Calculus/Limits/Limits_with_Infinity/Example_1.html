Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{x^2 + 1}{x - 2} }$
Solution
As $x$ approaches large values the constants in the numerator and denominator become insignificant:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{x^2 + 1}{x - 2} &= \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{x^2}{x} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} x \\ &= \infty }$
