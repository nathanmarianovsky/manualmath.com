Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} (3^x + 3^{2x})^{\frac{1}{x}} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} (3^x + 3^{2x})^{\frac{1}{x}} &= \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \Big( 3^{2x}(3^{-x} + 1) \Big)^{\frac{1}{x}} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} 9(1 + 3^{-x})^{\frac{1}{x}} \\ &= 9(1 + 0)^0 \\ &= 9 }$
