Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{3x^2}{4x^2 + 2x - 1} }$
Solution
As $x$ approaches large values the constant and linear factor in the denominator become insignificant:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{3x^2}{4x^2 + 2x - 1} &= \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{3x^2}{4x^2} \\ &= \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{3}{4} \\ &= \frac{3}{4} }$
