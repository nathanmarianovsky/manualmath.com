Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow -\infty} \frac{e^x}{4 + 5e^{3x}} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow -\infty} \frac{e^x}{4 + 5e^{3x}} &= \Big[ \lim_{x \rightarrow -\infty} e^x \Big] \Big[ \lim_{x \rightarrow -\infty} (4 + 5e^{3x}) \Big]^{-1} \\ &= (0)(4 + 5(0))^{-1} \\ &= 0 }$
