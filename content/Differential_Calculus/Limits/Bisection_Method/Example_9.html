Problem
Use the Bisection Method to approximate $\sqrt{2}$ up to $5$ decimal places at $x_5$, the fifth iteration.
Solution
The number of interest is a root of the function:
$\eqalign{ f(x) = x^2 - 2 }$
and looking at the graph:
shows that a suitable interval to restrict ourselves to is $[1,2]$. Next up the iterations take on the values:
$\textbf{n}$ $\textbf{x}_n$ $f(\textbf{x}_n)$
$0$ $1.50000$ $0.25000$
$1$ $1.25000$ $-0.43750$
$2$ $1.37500$ $-0.10937$
$3$ $1.43750$ $0.06641$
$4$ $1.40625$ $-0.02246$
$5$ $1.42188$ $0.02173$
