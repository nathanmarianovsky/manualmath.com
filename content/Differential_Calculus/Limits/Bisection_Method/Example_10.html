Problem
Use the Bisection Method to approximate $\sqrt{11}$ up to $5$ decimal places at $x_5$, the fifth iteration.
Solution
The number of interest is a root of the function:
$\eqalign{ f(x) = x^2 - 11 }$
and looking at the graph:
shows that a suitable interval to restrict ourselves to is $[3,4]$. Next up the iterations take on the values:
$\textbf{n}$ $\textbf{x}_n$ $f(\textbf{x}_n)$
$0$ $3.50000$ $1.25000$
$1$ $3.25000$ $-0.43750$
$2$ $3.37500$ $0.39063$
$3$ $3.31250$ $-0.02734$
$4$ $3.34375$ $0.18066$
$5$ $3.32813$ $0.07642$
