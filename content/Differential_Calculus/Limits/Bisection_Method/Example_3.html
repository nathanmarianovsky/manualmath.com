Problem
Given the equation:
$\eqalign{ \cos(x) = 2x }$
approximate the solution up to $5$ decimal places by using the Bisection Method to determine $x_5$, the fifth iteration, on the interval $[0,1]$.
Solution
First we rewrite the equation as:
$\eqalign{ 0 = \cos(x) - 2x }$
and identify the function of interest to be:
$\eqalign{ f(x) = \cos(x) - 2x }$
The iterations take on the values:
$\textbf{n}$ $\textbf{x}_n$ $f(\textbf{x}_n)$
$0$ $0.50000$ $-0.12242$
$1$ $0.25000$ $0.46891$
$2$ $0.37500$ $0.18051$
$3$ $0.43750$ $0.03081$
$4$ $0.46875$ $-0.04537$
$5$ $0.45313$ $-0.00717$
