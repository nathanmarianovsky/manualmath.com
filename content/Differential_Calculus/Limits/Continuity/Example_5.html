Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \ln\Big( \frac{e^{-x} + 25}{e^x + 5} \Big) }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \ln\Big( \frac{e^{-x} + 25}{e^x + 5} \Big) &= \ln\Big( \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{e^{-x} + 25}{e^x + 5} \Big) \\ &= \ln\Big( \frac{(0) + 25}{(0) + 5} \Big) \\ &= \ln(5) }$
