Problem
Evaluate:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \pi} e^{\cos\Big( x + \frac{\pi}{2} \Big)} }$
Solution
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \pi} e^{\cos\Big( x + \frac{\pi}{2} \Big)} &= e^{\lim_{x \rightarrow \pi} \cos\Big( x + \frac{\pi}{2} \Big)} \\ &= e^{\cos\Big( \lim_{x \rightarrow \pi} \Big( x + \frac{\pi}{2} \Big) \Big)} \\ &= e^{\cos\Big( \frac{3\pi}{2} \Big)} \\ &= e^0 \\ &= 1 }$
