Problem
Given that:
$\eqalign{ f(x) = \begin{cases} A + x & x < 2 \\ 1 + x^2 & x \geq 2 \end{cases} }$
Find a value for $A$ s.t. the function is continuous at the point $x = 2$.
Solution
To make the function continuous at $x = 2$ we want to equate the two components to each other at the point:
$\eqalign{ A + (2) &= 1 + (2)^2 \\ A &= 3 }$
