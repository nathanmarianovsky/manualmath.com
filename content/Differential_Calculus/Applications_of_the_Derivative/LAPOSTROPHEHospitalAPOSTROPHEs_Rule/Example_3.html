Problem
Evaluate the following using L'Hospital's Rule:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} xe^{-3x} }$
Solution
The initial limit will approach:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{x}{e^{3x}} = \frac{\infty}{\infty} }$
This provides reasoning to use L'Hospital's Rule:
$\eqalign{ \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{x}{e^{3x}} &= \lim_{x \rightarrow \infty} \frac{1}{3e^{3x}} \\ &= 0 }$
