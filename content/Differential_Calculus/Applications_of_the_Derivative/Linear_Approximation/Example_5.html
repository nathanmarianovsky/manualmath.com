Problem
Approximate:
$\eqalign{ \sqrt{1.1} }$
using an appropriate function and center. What is the error up to $5$ decimal places?
Solution
With the given setup we can conclude that a good function to choose is:
$\eqalign{ f(x) = \sqrt{x} }$
which can be approximated near $x = 1$ as:
$\eqalign{ f(x) &\approx f(1) + f'(1)(x - 1) \\ &= 1 + \frac{1}{2}(x - 1) }$
So we approximate the setup as:
$\eqalign{ \sqrt{1.1} &= f(1.1) \\ &\approx 1 + \frac{1}{2}(1.1 - 1) \\ &= 1.05 }$
which gives the error:
$\eqalign{ \Delta &= \Big| f(x) - \Big( 1 + \frac{1}{2}(x - 1) \Big) \Big| \\ &= |f(1.1) - 1.05| \\ &= |1.04881 - 1.05000| \\ &= 0.00119 }$
