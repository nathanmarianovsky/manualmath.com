Problem
Given:
$\eqalign{ f(2) = 5 \hspace{.3cm} \text{and} \hspace{.3cm} f'(2) = 3 }$
approximate $f(1.9)$ and $f(2.1)$.
Solution
With the given information we write down the linear approximation as:
$\eqalign{ f(x) &\approx f(2) + f'(2)(x - 2) \\ &= 5 + 3(x - 2) }$
So now by direct computation:
$\eqalign{ f(1.9) &\approx 5 + 3(1.9 - 2) \\ &= 4.7 \\ f(2.1) &\approx 5 + 3(2.1 - 2) \\ &= 5.3 }$
