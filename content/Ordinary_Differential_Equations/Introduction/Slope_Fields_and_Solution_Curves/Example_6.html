Problem
Given the differential equation:
$\eqalign{ \frac{dy}{dx} = x }$
use the First Fundamental Theorem of Calculus to find the general solution and sketch some of the solution curves.
Solution
In order to find the solution:
$\eqalign{ y(x) &= \int \frac{dy}{dx} dx \\ &= \int x dx \\ &= \frac{1}{2}x^2 + C }$
