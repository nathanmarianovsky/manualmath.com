Problem
Find all solutions to:
$\eqalign{ \frac{dy}{dx} - x^2y^2 = x^2 }$
Solution
Since the function is separable:
$\eqalign{ \frac{dy}{dx} - x^2y^2 &= x^2 \\ \frac{dy}{dx} &= x^2y^2 + x^2 \\ \frac{dy}{dx} &= x^2(y^2 + 1) \\ \frac{dy}{y^2 + 1} &= x^2 dx \\ \int \frac{dy}{y^2 + 1} &= \int x^2 dx \\ \arctan(y) &= \frac{x^3}{3} + C \\ \arctan(y) - \frac{x^3}{3} &= C }$
The above solution could have been put in explicit form using simple algebraic manipulation:
$\eqalign{ \arctan(y) - \frac{x^3}{3} &= C \\ \arctan(y) &= \frac{x^3}{3} + C \\ y &= \tan\Big( \frac{x^3}{3} + C \Big) }$
