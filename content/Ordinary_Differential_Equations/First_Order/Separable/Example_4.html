Problem
Find all solutions to:
$\eqalign{ \frac{dy}{dt} = 1 + \frac{1}{y^2} }$
Solution
Since the function is separable:
$\eqalign{ \frac{dy}{dt} &= 1 + \frac{1}{y^2} \\ \frac{dy}{1 + y^{-2}} &= dt \\ \int \frac{dy}{1 + y^{-2}} &= \int dt \\ \int \frac{y^2}{y^2 + 1} dy &= t + C \\ \int \Big( 1 - \frac{1}{y^2 + 1} \Big) dy &= t + C \\ y - \arctan(y) &= t + C \\ y - \arctan(y) - t &= C }$
The above scenario does not provide any simple means of obtaining the explicit solution, therefore just stick with the implicit solution.
