Notation

Here you will find all the notation used throughout the study of ordinary differential equations and its corresponding meaning:
$y'$, $y'(x)$, $\frac{dy}{dx}$ The first derivative of $y$ with respect to $x$, and with this patten having $n$ apostrophes corresponds to the $n$th derivative
$y^{(n)}$, $y^{(n)}(x)$, $\frac{d^ny}{dx^n}$ The $n$th derivative of $y$ with respect to $x$
